# Two-Wheeler Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes sales performance for a two-wheeler dealership across multiple motorcycle segments including 400cc, Big Bikes, Adventure Bikes, and Cruisers.

The objective is to monitor dealership performance through an interactive Power BI dashboard and identify insights related to sales growth, revenue contribution, and profitability.

---

## Business Objective

Dealership management required a dashboard to track:

- Sales performance by segment
- Revenue trends
- Profit margins
- Monthly sales growth
- Top-performing motorcycle categories

The goal was to enable **data-driven decision making** for inventory planning and marketing strategies.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains sales transactions across different motorcycle segments.

### Key Fields

- Date
- Motorcycle Segment
- Units Sold
- Price
- Revenue
- Profit Margin
- Profit

---

## Dashboard KPIs

The Power BI dashboard tracks the following metrics:

- Total Units Sold
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Sales by Segment
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Profit Contribution by Segment

---

## Key Insights

- Big Bikes generated the highest revenue due to higher price points.
- The 400cc segment had the highest volume of sales.
- Adventure bikes showed strong growth during touring seasons.
- Cruisers contributed steady profits due to stable demand.
